About this Chi bobolink

a creative bio

"What matters most is how well you walk through the fire.
- Charles Bukowski

a dreamer

Jamie dreams of a world where teleportation is a reality for many sensible reasons, but is perhaps most excited that it would forever do away with the anxiety that comes from having no idea which direction she is facing when she leaves a train station. That might, in part, explain her current home in Logan Square, a mere 200 steps from the Blue Line.

a doer

As a graphic designer for more than 10 years, Jamie has enjoyed working closely with clients to help them visually represent their business. In spite of working from home during that time, she's charmingly unfamiliar with the vast reaches of daytime television. She can’t properly identify a single reality-tv star. If she is watching something, chances are it’s either animated or pure Geek.

a reader

To say that she likes books is an understatement. Literature is a guiding light and among her most beloved forms of escape from reality; which just isn’t always aesthetically pleasing enough to satisfy her.

a quirk

When Jamie isn't creating beautiful things with her computer, she's bringing those skills to the physical world by way of her innate ability for positioning baubles and junk in eye-pleasing arrangements.

She also dreams vivid dreams of floating coasters, digital nail polish and a TARDIS.
